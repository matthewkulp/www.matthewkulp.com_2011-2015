Munich Chair

January 1, 2012 / ash, plastic, hardware

Inspired by the design traditions of Germany and Austria, this chair is a cross between contemporary design studios like Nitzan Cohen and KGID and classic ones like Thonet. The result is a beautiful and minimal chair that unscrews to ship flat.
