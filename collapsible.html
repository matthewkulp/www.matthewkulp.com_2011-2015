Collapsible

January 20, 2011 / oak, maple, hardware

The legs rotate around a pivot point hidden from view. The seat attaches to an arm that pivots independently around a point located on the outermost leg. The result is a work stool that can hang on a wall or an edge of a table, tuck neatly into a closet or under a bed and ship flat, fully assembled.
