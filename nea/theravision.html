Theravision

Circuit Bending, Product Design.

A television modified to act as an oscilloscope receives signal from a theramin. Housed in one unit, the theravision reacts to users visually and audibly based on their proximity to two aluminum arms. The result is a synesthetic experience that is intuitive and engaging.
